Pig Dice Game

Hello! Would you like to play a game of Pig Dice? The rules are simple.

Rules:

  • This is a 2 player game
  • On a player's turn, they roll a die
  • If they roll a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn
  • If the roll is anything else, the player adds it to their turn total
  • They have 2 choices at this point:
    • "Hold", which means their turn total is added to their score and the turn passes to the next player
    • Roll again. If they have points in their turn total and roll a 1, they forfeit those points (cannot be added to score) and pass their turn to the next player. Repeat above steps.
  • The game is won when a player has a score of 100
