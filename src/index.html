PIG DICE

RULES

  • Each player rolls the dice continuously until they roll 1 or choose to "hold".
  • If the roll is not 1, their bank (i.e. score for that turn) is increased by the roll value. At that point they may roll again or "hold."
  • If "hold," that player's total score is increased by bank amount and it's the next player's turn.
  • If player rolls a 1, their bank is reset to 0 and it is the next player's turn.
  • The second player repeats the previous steps until one player reaches a score of 100.
